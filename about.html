Brand story

Designing spaces that breathe.

Novelle Interiors was founded on a simple belief: luxury should quiet the noise of life, not add to it. Our work blends warm minimalism with layered materiality, creating interiors that feel refined, personal, and deeply livable.

Lead designer

Elena Vale

Elena brings a background in spatial planning, textile study, and boutique hospitality design. Her rooms are known for graceful restraint: sculptural silhouettes, tactile finishes, and a disciplined sense of calm.

Philosophy

Three principles guide every decision.

01

Simplicity

Clear plans, edited palettes, and fewer objects with stronger intention.

02

Elegance

Quiet details, natural textures, and proportions that feel harmonious over time.

03

Functionality

Rooms shaped around real movement, real storage, and the life already unfolding there.
